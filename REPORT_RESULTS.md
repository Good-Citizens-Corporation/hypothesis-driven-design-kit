# Share Your Results

We're collecting community outcomes to validate whether hypothesis-driven design works in practice.

---

## How to Submit

**[Submit via GitHub Issue →](https://github.com/Good-Citizens-Corporation/hypothesis-driven-design-kit/issues/new/choose)**

## What We Want to Know

### About Your Experiment

- **Team/Organization:** (can be anonymous)
- **Domain:** (software, research, design, other)
- **PRE-ID(s) tested:** How many hypotheses did you pre-register?

### Outcomes

| Question | Options |
|----------|---------|
| Did you complete at least one full loop? | Yes / No / In progress |
| Were any hypotheses falsified? | Yes / No |
| Were you surprised by any results? | Yes / No |

### Learnings

- **What surprised you?** (most valuable)
- **What would you change about the process?**
- **Would you recommend HDD to other teams?**

### Optional

- Link to your public evidence packet (if comfortable sharing)
- Screenshots of interesting findings
- Quotes from team members

---

## Why We're Collecting This

We're testing our own hypothesis:

> **PRE-META-001:** The HDD process will surface at least one unexpected finding per team that tries it, and >70% of teams will report they would recommend it.

**Falsification criteria:**
- <50% of teams report surprises
- <50% would recommend
- No teams complete a full loop

Your data helps us validate (or falsify) whether this methodology works.
